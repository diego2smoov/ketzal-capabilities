# Ketzal Defense — Alternative Headlines

Current headline in use:
> “You win the contract. We make sure it works.”

Below are 6 refined headline options. Each is written to work on the landing page (H1) and can also serve as the lead line in the Defense Ecosystem one-pager.

## Recommended Alternatives

### 1. Strongest Overall Recommendation
**“We close the operational credibility gap in every drone warfare proposal.”**

Why it works: Directly names the core pain (credibility gap) that primes face, positions Ketzal as the solution, and feels pre- and post-award relevant.

### 2. Very Strong Runner-Up
**“You have the contract. Now you need operators who’ve actually done it.”**

Why it works: Sharp, conversational, and highlights the lived experience gap better than the current version. Builds immediate tension.

### 3. Best for Primes & Integrators
**“Turn your drone hardware bid into a credible, operator-backed proposal.”**

Why it works: Speaks directly to bid teams and proposal writers. Emphasizes “credibility” in the eyes of the customer.

### 4. Operator-First Positioning
**“Combat-proven operators embedded in your team — not in your proposal.”**

Why it works: Distinguishes Ketzal from pure consultants. Emphasizes real operators who will actually show up and deliver.

### 5. Slightly More Premium / Consultative
**“The integration layer that turns drone hardware into real warfighting capability.”**

Why it works: Elevates Ketzal from “training vendor” to strategic capability partner. Works well with defense primes.

### 6. Shorter, Punchier Option (for email subject or hero)
**“Real operators. Real TTPs. Real results for your drone contract.”**
