3–5 MAY 2026 • MIAMI, FL

Miami Maritime RED TEAM — NCIS HUMINT + cUAS Calibration

Simulated water-to-beach FPV attacks from a fast boat onto beachside park targets to expose gaps ahead of FIFA 2026 venues. 5-inch analog test flight confirmed the obvious in the worst possible way: standard ELRS + single-antenna video link dies in salt spray, multipath, and crowded 2.4 GHz. Pilot brought it home visual — no airframe lost. Two-man doctrine (pilot + mission coordinator) proved mandatory in real time for deconfliction and reaction.

Immediate SOP change: GPS on every airframe before it leaves the boat, digital video or Gemini diversity for anything that matters, buoyancy and RTL failsafes non-negotiable for over-water work. The cUAS systems on site got exactly the demonstration they needed — and a clear picture of what they were still blind to.
